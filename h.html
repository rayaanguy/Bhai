Welcome to city of joy

Kolkata, formerly Calcutta, is the vibrant capital of West Bengal and widely regarded as the cultural capital of India. Located on the east bank of the Hooghly River, this "City of Joy" serves as a major commercial and intellectual hub of eastern India. It is renowned for its rich history, colonial-era architecture, profound literature, artistic heritage, and grand Durga Puja celebrations.
